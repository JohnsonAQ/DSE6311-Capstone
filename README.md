## Research Question

Can patient vital signs be used to accurately predict which patients will require supplemental oxygen during hospitalization 
or while receiving other clinical care?

## Hypothesis 

H0: Patient vital signs do not predict whether a patient will require supplemental oxygen.
H1: Patient vital signs can predict whether a patient will require supplemental oxygen.

## Prediction 

Using routinely collected patient vitals, a model can be developed to predict whether a hospitalized 
patient or patient in another clinical setting will require supplemental oxygen (Yes/No). 
In developing such a model, at-risk patients can be identified early, setting the stage for any necessary, 
timely intervention by healthcare providers, and improving patient outcomes.
